About Us Please use the form lower down to get in touch with us. What is Cardiac Osris Foundation? Cardiac Osiris Foundation is a Non-Profit Organisation (NPO) founded in July 2016 and it is registered with the Department of Trade and Industry. A NGO run by people sharing the same interests. We help needy people with their basic needs, such as employment, food and social development.we identifies the following aspects as being at being a provider for needy people, amongst others issues : Poverty, starvation, humiliation, degradation, inequality and unemployment. improving the quality of life. We dream of a South Africa in which each person’s dignity is equally valued and respected.

COF’s approach to service delivery is encapsulated in the following concepts: “Batho Pele” – People First * Consultation: we need to understand your needs in order to recommend solutions * Service Standards: we strive for excellence * Access: we strive to make our services available to people from all backgrounds and all levels of employment * Courtesy and care: we care for people and treat them with courtesy * Openness and Transparency: what you see is what you get * Redress: we are flexible and can respond maturely
